Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about the 369 manifestation method and our app

What is the 369 manifestation method?

The 369 method is a manifestation technique inspired by Nikola Tesla's belief in the power of 3, 6, and 9. You write your affirmation 3 times in the morning, 6 times in the afternoon, and 9 times before bed to amplify your manifestation power.

How long does it take to see results?

Results vary for each person, but many users report significant shifts in their reality within 33-45 days of consistent practice. Some notice smaller changes even sooner. The key is consistency and belief in the process.

Do I need to use your app to practice the 369 method?

No, you can practice the 369 method with just pen and paper. However, our app provides structure, reminders, affirmation generation, progress tracking, and community support that significantly enhances your practice and improves consistency.

Is the free version enough to get started?

Absolutely! The free version includes all the essential features you need to practice the 369 method effectively. Premium features enhance your experience but aren't required for successful manifestation.

Can I manifest multiple wishes at once?

While you can work on multiple wishes, we recommend focusing on 1-3 wishes at a time for maximum effectiveness. This helps maintain focus and clarity in your manifestation practice.
