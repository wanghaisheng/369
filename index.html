Frequently Asked Questions

Why is it called the 369 method?

The method is named after Nikola Tesla's belief that 3, 6, and 9 are powerful numbers in the universe. It also refers to the practice pattern: writing affirmations 3 times in the morning, 6 times in the afternoon, and 9 times in the evening.

How long does it take to see results?

Results vary by individual and the nature of your desire. Many users report seeing initial signs within 33 days, while others complete a 45-day cycle for more significant manifestations. Consistency is key to success.

Can I manifest multiple desires at once?

While it's possible to work on multiple manifestations, we recommend focusing on one primary desire at a time for beginners. This helps maintain clear focus and energy. As you become more experienced, you can expand to multiple practices.

What if I miss a day of practice?

Consistency is important, but missing a day doesn't invalidate your practice. Simply resume the next day and maintain your positive intention. Our app helps you stay on track with gentle reminders and streak protection features.
