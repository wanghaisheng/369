My Manifestation Journey

How the 369 method helped me overcome 38 years of self-blame and inspired me to help others

From the Founder

For 38 years, I carried the weight of self-blame on my shoulders. Every setback, every failure, every missed opportunity became another brick in the wall I built around myself. I was my own harshest critic, and this constant self-judgment left me feeling stuck, worthless, and powerless to change my circumstances.

The Turning Point: Discovering Manifestation

My turning point came when I discovered manifestation techniques, particularly the 369 method. At first, I was skeptical. How could simply writing affirmations change my deeply ingrained patterns of self-blame? But I was desperate for change, so I committed to trying it consistently for 45 days.

The affirmation I chose was simple: "I am worthy of love and forgiveness, especially from myself." Three times in the morning, six times in the afternoon, and nine times before bed – I wrote these words faithfully, even on days when I didn't believe them.

The Transformation

The change wasn't immediate, but it was profound. Around day 21, I noticed my internal dialogue beginning to shift. The voice of self-blame grew quieter, while a new voice – one of compassion and understanding – grew stronger. By day 45, I had experienced a fundamental shift in how I related to myself.

This wasn't just positive thinking – it was a rewiring of deeply held beliefs. The 369 method had helped me create new neural pathways, ones that led to self-acceptance rather than self-destruction. For the first time in 38 years, I felt free from the prison of self-blame.

The Mission: Creating HeyManifestation.com

My personal transformation sparked a mission: to make manifestation techniques accessible to everyone struggling with similar challenges. I founded HeyManifestation.com with a bold vision – to create 50 different manifestation apps, each addressing specific areas of life where people feel stuck or limited.

The 369 Manifestation Method app is the first of these 50 planned applications. I designed it to provide structure, accountability, and guidance – everything I wish I'd had when starting my own journey. The app combines ancient wisdom with modern technology to make manifestation practice simple, effective, and accessible.

My Invitation to You

If you're carrying the weight of self-blame, limiting beliefs, or any other mental barriers that keep you from living your fullest life, I invite you to try the 369 method. Whether you use our app or simply a pen and paper, the consistent practice of rewriting your mental narrative can create profound change.

And as I work toward my goal of creating 50 manifestation apps, I welcome your feedback, suggestions, and stories. Together, we can build tools that help more people break free from self-imposed limitations and step into their full potential.

With gratitude for being part of this journey,

The Founder of HeyManifestation.com
